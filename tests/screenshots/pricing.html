Questions, answered

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing portal in one click — your access continues until the end of the current period.

What happens after the free trial?

We'll email you a few days before the trial ends. If you do nothing, the trial cancels and you stay on the Free plan.

Do you store my CVs?

Yes, in your account, encrypted at rest. You can export or delete everything from your dashboard at any time.

Will the AI invent things on my CV?

No. The model rewrites and reorders what's already on your profile to match the job. It never fabricates jobs, dates, or metrics.
